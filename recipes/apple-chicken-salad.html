Chicken Granny Smith Salad

Description

A description of the Chicken Granny Smith Salad should go here. Someday.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Strips
  • A mix of rocca, arugula and basil
  • 2 small Granny Smith apples, diced
  • 160g Snow Peas
  • A handfull of Walnuts, crushed
  • Emmental, cut into small cubes
  • 1 Lemon
  • 1 tbsp Butter
  • 2 tbsp Pesto
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Chili Flakes (optional)

Steps

  1. Season the chicken strips with salt, pepper, garlic powder, chili flakes (optional) and lemon zest. Let rest in the fridge for 30 minutes
  2. Heat up a pan and add butter. Once melted add in the chicken strips and cook both sides
  3. When the chicken is cooked through, add juice of half of lemon, mix and remove chicken from pan
  4. Immediately add the cut snow peas into the pan, mix well and let cook, stiring occasionally. Once done, put the cooked snow peas aside a let cool
  5. For the sauce, mix pesto, olive oil and half a lemon in a bowl, add salt, pepper and chili flakes to taste
  6. In a salad bowl, add in the mix of Aragula, rocca and Basil, the granny smith, cubed emmental and the snow peas
  7. Top with the chicken strips, the pesto sauce and crushed walnuts
