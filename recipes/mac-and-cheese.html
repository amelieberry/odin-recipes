Mac and Cheese

Description

Imagine this a descriptive paragraph about Mac and Cheese. It would have many words describing the cheesy dish. But I can't think of any, so let's get to the reason you're here and keep scrolling.

Ingredients

  • 230g pasta of choice
  • 200ml cream or 150g cream cheese
  • Go nuts on the shredded cheese (Good choices: Parmesan, Kashkaval, Gouda, Cheddar, Gruyere)
  • 1 tsp Dijon Mustard
  • 1 pinch nutmeg powder
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 large tbsp butter
  • Optional: 250g mushrooms or 100g corn
  • Fresh thyme
  • 200g cherry tomatoes
  • Pumpkin seeds
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Cut the cherry tomatoes in half, roast them at 180°C for 30 minutes with salt, pepper, garlic powder and oregano.
  2. Roast pumpkin seeds at 200°C for 10 minutes.
  3. Cook pasta as indicated on packaging. If using fresh pasta, cook till they float.
  4. Heat butter and garlic in a pan, stir till very light brown.
  5. Add Dijon mustard.
  6. Add mushrooms (optional).
  7. Pour in the cream or cream cheese, let simmer and add some pasta water if needed.
  8. Season with nutmeg powder, fresh thyme, salt and pepper.
  9. Add the shredded cheese and mix.
  10. Once the pasta is cooked, tranfer them to the cream sauce with some pasta water as needed. Mix well and serve.
